The Spores

The Spores are all of the offspring of Molly. We call them this because one of Molly's nicknames is Moldy, thus her children are her spores. We adopted all of the spores because a family friend had to move in with us for a few months, and all of their cats came with us. When they eventually moved out, they could only take a few of their original cats with them, and the rest is history.
